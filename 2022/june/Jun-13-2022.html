Low-Fidelity Wireframes
We released a new Figma library called "Low-Fidelity Wireframes." This library's purpose is for designers to explore and share ideas by creating quick mock-ups. Wireframing is helpful during early stages of a project, or during a pivot. Wireframing is also helpful for collaborative brainstorming among UX team members. See the library and learn more here.
Instant Figma library access
If you had a hard time figuring out what library to turn on, this update is for you!
We now automatically turn on libraries based on what team a designer creates their file in.
Desktop designers creating files under the Firefox Desktop team now get instant access to all desktop libraries. Mobile designers creating files under the Firefox Mobile team now get instant access to all mobile libraries.
If you want to turn on certain libraries for your personal drafts, you can learn to do that here.
